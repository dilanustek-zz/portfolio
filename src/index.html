ABOUT ME

I'm an MSc student interested in the way humans interact with computers and designing better technology with the human in mind.

Designer

Developer

Researcher

I started my human-computer interaction design journey by specializing in HCI at UBC as part of my master's in Computer Science. I've done projects focusing on interaction design and user-experience design. My current thesis is designing mid-air interactions using proximity sensors.

I received my Bachelor in Computer Science from Grinnell College during which I interned at Goldman Sachs twice. After I graduated, I worked for Monitise EMEA, at the Istanbul office to develop mobile banking and e-commerce apps. My public repos can be found on Github!

Research is an essential skill both when designing usable products with a pleasant experience, and building the right product for people. My research publications are found on my resume but here's the most important: my master's thesis.
