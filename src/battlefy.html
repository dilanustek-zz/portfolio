Integrating a New Game

Problem

To integrate a globally popular game, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, we identified two main user needs for this game:

  1. When players check-in to their match, they need to perform a real-time map veto process to identify which maps they will play on.
  2. When map veto process is done, tournament admins need to quickly provide information to the players in a match on how to get into their game so that the players can start competing.

Solution

After players check-in, they are guided to perform their map veto in real time. Team captains take turns to veto a map until there are 3 maps left in a game that is Best-of-3. In this solution, we not only delivered the functionality, but we also delighted users by animating the veto process to make it more fun and visually pleasing for their viewers if they are streaming their game online.

Impact

The day after we launched, a global tournament with a $300,000 prize pool was run the next day. The tournament ran for 3 months and had 16 tournaments. There were 250,000+ page-views a month for this tournament.
